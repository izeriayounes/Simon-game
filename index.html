Here are the basic instructions on how to play the Simon game:

  • Turn on the game by pressing the start button.
  • Simon (the game) will light up one of the four colored buttons, and play a sound.
  • Repeat the same sequence by pressing the same colored button.
  • Simon will add one more color to the sequence and repeat the sequence again.
  • Continue to repeat the sequence until you make a mistake.
  • If you make a mistake, the game will end and you will have to start over from the beginning.
  • If you can successfully repeat a sequence of 20 colors, you win the game!
